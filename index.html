    Who we are

    CALIFORNIA STATE LICENSED CONTRACTOR

    You can count on years of trouble-free security with the Baby Barrier Pool Safety Fence. All poles, fittings, and mesh are manufactured to resist rust, mildew, corrosion, rot and fading. All hardware is made from durable brass or stainless steel. The UV stabilized materials are ideally suited to withstand high-moisture, guard against salt water and pool chemical environments. Our fence poles have undergone a horizontal force load test with a strength of greater than 75 pounds. A limited lifetime manufacturer’s warranty provides you with peace of mind for many years to come.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Is it a problem if we have level (elevation) changes around the pool?

      No Problem!

    • Does the fence come in different colors?

      The fence comes in black, white and brown.

    • How long does the installation take?

      Depends on the layout, but installation occurs within the same day.

    • What are the guidelines for the pool fence to pass inspection?

      The fence needs to be installed 2 feet from the perimeter of the pool. Other guidelines concerning safety regulations apply, but this is taken care of by the product itself.
