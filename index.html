    ABOUT

    Baby Barrier Pool Fence originated in 1963, in Florida. Today we offer the strongest and the safest product in the industry.

    We are owner-operated and have been installing Baby Barrier Pool Fences locally for over 25 Years.

    You can count on years of trouble-free security with the Baby Barrier Pool Fence:

    • All poles, fittings, and mesh are manufactured to resist rust, mildew, corrosion, rot and fading.
    • All hardware is made from durable brass or stainless steel.
    • The UV stabilized materials are ideally suited to withstand high-moisture, guard against salt water and pool chemical environments.
    • Fence poles have undergone a horizontal force load test with a strength of greater than 75 pounds.
    • 10 Year Prorated manufacturer’s warranty provides you with peace of mind for many years to come.

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Does the fence come in different colors?

      The fence comes in black, white and brown.

    • How long does the installation take?

      Depending on the layout, typically installation occurs within the same day.

    • What are the guidelines for the pool fence to pass inspection?

      The fence needs to be a minimum of 4' high and have a self closing gate. The fence needs to enclose all access to the pool. All Other guidelines concerning safety regulations apply, and our product meets or exceeds ASTM F-2286-05

    • Is it a problem if we have level (elevation) changes around the pool?

      Each fence is custom tailored and level changes are our specialty.
