Self Closing Gate

A remarkable gate design pioneered by Baby Barrier® (as seen on Good Morning America), not only closes by itself, it latches automatically.

This revolutionary design provides superior latching capability by virtually eliminating the mechanical friction resistance which so often causes failure in other designs. It is the strongest gate in the industry, making it the most reliable for keeping kids safer.

The closing tension is adjustable and gate latches have undergone rigorous testing through 50,000 openings and closings to help ensure reliability. The gate is also key-lockable and the entire gate could be removed whenever the fence is taken down.

  • Self Closing, Latches automatically.
  • Industry leader in durability and function.
  • Lockable using key.
